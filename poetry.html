Vena Cava


Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!

I feel like the times are coming back,
Repeating themselves in such a way
That terrifies me!

Pieces of everyone I knew
Return in every field of vision.
No one can see it!

The rapids of this bleeding river
Feel like they're gonna fall,
And then that will be all.

This play is serendipity in reverse,
All according to plan, let's rehearse.
I just know that the final product
Will be the game to end it all!


Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!

Silence! The followers are speaking.
The saviour has returned to torture us,
Freeing us from the depths of free will.


Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!

Fallacious arguments about fallacies,
“It won't return that way, it can't be true.”
All I see is you denying reality!

Peace will never break into my mind,
And love will never enter my vena cava!
See the timeline!

Do I make myself clear enough,
When I tell you I'm not getting better?
Cede the timeline!

This play is serendipity in reverse,
All according to plan, let's rehearse.
I just know that the final product
Will be the game to end it all!


Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!


Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!

Hope will never enter my brain,
And love will never enter my vena cava!
