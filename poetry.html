On Prisons (Interlude I)

Can you hear the bleeding?
Can you feel the screaming?
They want out of containment.
Torture isn't valid payment.

Young people are driven here,
Through a system that puts them here.
It's not their fault to fight,
They have been denied life.

No one should be left alone like this.
Do you know what happens here?
Violence breeds more violence,
Don't you know that this is what that is?

Young people are driven here,
They haven't learned what peace is.
It's not their fault to fight,
And you made them angrier.

False Advertising (Scout's Theme)

I feel the heart rotting in my chest.
The stains and bandages across it,
Seem to read my name, and they say:

“There's no coming back from this illness,
Your heart will degrade forever.
Everything you want to love,
Is so far out of reach you'll hate it.”

Ten lifetimes of torture,
Ten lifetimes of waiting,
Ten lifetimes of violence,
This is how murderers are made.


I've gotten my lifetime's worth.
This fucking movie is old news,
But now I have to continue.
What is in the heart of these others?
Love, peace, and fucking happiness?
That's what I was sold on,
But it's so clearly false advertising.

I feel like all I can do in this film,
Is what I've always gotten.
Ten lifetimes worth of uselessness,
And ten lifetimes worth of violence.

This movie is old news now,
And I thought it would be over.
What kind of film has no end?
What kind of film does the protagonist
Keep getting shot over and over?

It won't be this film, I'll tell you.

Vena Cava

Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!

I feel like the times are coming back,
Repeating themselves in such a way
That terrifies me!

Pieces of everyone I knew
Return in every field of vision.
No one can see it!

The rapids of this bleeding river
Feel like they're gonna fall,
And then that will be all.

This play is serendipity in reverse,
All according to plan, let's rehearse.
I just know that the final product
Will be the game to end it all!


Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!

Silence! The followers are speaking.
The saviour has returned to torture us,
Freeing us from the depths of free will.


Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!

Fallacious arguments about fallacies,
“It won't return that way, it can't be true.”
All I see is you denying reality!

Peace will never break into my mind,
And love will never enter my vena cava!
See the timeline!

Do I make myself clear enough,
When I tell you I'm not getting better?
Cede the timeline!

This play is serendipity in reverse,
All according to plan, let's rehearse.
I just know that the final product
Will be the game to end it all!


Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!


Again, again!
Again, again!
Again, again, again, again!

Hope will never enter my brain,
And love will never enter my vena cava!
