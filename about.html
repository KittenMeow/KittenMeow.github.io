About Me

Hello! I am Autumn Percy, the writer and creator of News on the Neuse. I am a freshman at Fuquay-Varina High School, and I am fifteen years old. I am working on many projects, and you can find out about them here!

Capable Yet Large Computer

The Capable Yet Large Computer (CYLC, pronounced silk) is a computer that I am working on. I am first using breadboards to create a model of the first version, then improving it, and then it will be on PCB.
I am in the planning stages of CYLC, and I have ordered the first parts.

A Short History of Languages

A Short History of Languages is a video series I have been working on making. I have only begun to write the first script for the Germanic langauges. I will begin with the Proto-Germanic language and then discuss the evolution from there to each living Germanic language.

Language Learning

I am learning German and Finnish mostly, however I sometimes will dabble in Swedish, Danish, Georgian, French, and other languages.
