News on the Neuse

Introduction

Hello! I am Autumn Percy, and this is News on the Neuse. I am setting out to write a newspaper for North Carolina, both talking about politics and about other things.

I originally planned this to be a high school newspaper, however I expanded the idea to all of North Carolina.

So welcome to Neuse on the News! See you around.
